About Time Lock

Welcome to Time Lock! Our app empowers you to create secure, time-delayed messages that can only be accessed after a set period. This feature is particularly useful for storing sensitive information such as passwords for app or website blockers, ensuring that you have a cooldown period to help prevent unwanted access.

Whether you're looking to boost your productivity by limiting distractions or just want an extra layer of security for your confidential data, Time Lock provides a simple and effective solution. Experience the peace of mind that comes with knowing your information is both safe and temporally protected.
